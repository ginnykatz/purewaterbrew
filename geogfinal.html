About

This story map describes the story of a beer made from purified, local effluent, and the journey of Clean Water Services of Hillsboro, OR.

This Geog 4572 presentation was reformatted from the original product that was created for Clean Water Service's presentation for Singapore International Water Week. In this version, we highlight the different data displays and story telling methods our team created to convey the Pure Water Brew conversation.
